Leagues

Split users into "groups" when they log in, based on timestamp. When 10 users sign up, add them to the same group, all with the same number of points: ZERO.


When a user gains points, they rank higher than other users. Then, after a while (1 day), they are moved to another "group" based on criteria such as:


"Your score the highest in your group, and 5% higher than the second highest score, so we are moving you to a superior group".



The terminology of leagues / groups will need to be analysed further.
